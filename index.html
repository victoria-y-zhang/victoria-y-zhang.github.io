About Me

I am a fourth year PhD candidate in the Economic Sociology Program at Northeastern University where I work with Cristina Nita-Rotaru. My research has broadened over the course of my PhD. Generally speaking, I'm interested in the security of internet protocols. My research has mostly focused on validating the correctness of security-aware protocols from DNSSEC to SDN to the wild west of the blockchain space.


I am an engineer by training, and worked as a software engineer for five years at both IBM and a since-purchased Silicon Valley startup.
